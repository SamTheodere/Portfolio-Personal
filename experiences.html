Seasonal Guide

Best Time to Experience Us

🌸
Spring
February — April

Wildflowers in bloom, cool mornings, perfect for outdoor yoga, farm walks, and cycling. Best for families and wellness seekers.

Best for: Farm visits · Yoga retreats · Cycling
🌿
Monsoon
June — September

The farm comes alive. Waterfalls roar, the land turns emerald. Our most atmospheric season — ideal for photography, spa stays, and quiet retreats.

Best for: Spa detox · Photography · Solitude
🍂
Harvest Season
October — December

The finest weather and the most bountiful farm harvests. Our chef's table, farm dinners, and outdoor events are at their absolute best.

Best for: Farm dining · Events · Stargazing
❄️
Winter Peak
December — January

Coolest temperatures, clearest skies, and a festive atmosphere across all properties. The busiest and most celebratory time of year. Book early.

Best for: Couples · Celebrations · Stargazing
