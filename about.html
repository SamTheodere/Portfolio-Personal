How It Began

The Aranyakaa Origin

It began with a single walk through a forest in Ramanagara in 2018. Our founders — a husband-and-wife team with backgrounds in sustainable architecture and culinary arts — were searching for land to build a weekend home. What they found instead was a calling.

The 42-acre plot of degraded agricultural land near Ramanagara's rocky hills was barren, eroded, and overlooked. But they saw possibility. Over the next 14 months, they worked with local farmers, ecologists, and artisans to revive the land — planting 8,000 native trees, restoring the soil with organic farming, and constructing the first three cottages using mud, stone, and bamboo sourced entirely within a 30 km radius.

"We didn't want to build a resort on nature. We wanted to restore nature and invite people to witness it."

Club Aranyakaa opened its gates in January 2019 with just 6 rooms and a bonfire pit. Within six months, it was fully booked three months in advance. The concept had struck a chord — people were desperate for a kind of luxury that felt honest, rooted, and alive.

Today, we manage five properties across South India, with two more in development. But our philosophy remains unchanged: every property we build must give back more to the earth than it takes.

What We Stand For

Our Philosophy

At Club Aranyakaa, we believe that the most luxurious thing in the world is an uninterrupted connection with nature. Every decision we make — from construction materials to kitchen sourcing — is filtered through four core principles that define who we are.

🌿

Ecological First

Every property is built with minimal ecological disruption. We restore, not impose. Our construction philosophy: if nature didn't put it there, think twice.

🤝

Community Rooted

Over 85% of our staff are from within 20 km of each property. We source ingredients locally, buy crafts locally, and return profits locally.

✨

Uncompromised Comfort

Sustainability is not an excuse for discomfort. We refuse to choose between ecology and luxury — we insist on both, unapologetically.

🔄

Regenerative Design

We aim for each property to be net-positive — generating more energy, growing more food, and sequestering more carbon than it consumes.

Our Commitment

Sustainability at
Every Level

☀️

100% Renewable Energy

All Club Aranyakaa properties are powered exclusively by on-site solar installations. We generate more power than we consume and contribute surplus to the local grid.

580 kWpSolar capacity across all properties
💧

Water Stewardship

Rainwater harvesting systems, greywater recycling, and natural bioswales ensure we consume 60% less water than a conventional resort of comparable size.

60%Water consumption reduction
🌾

Zero Food Miles

80% of all food served across our restaurants is grown within the property or sourced from farmers within a 15 km radius. No cold chains, no plastic packaging.

80%Farm-to-table ingredient sourcing
♻️

Zero Waste Operations

Composting, biogas digesters, and a circular materials economy ensure that less than 2% of our operational waste reaches a landfill across all properties.

<2%Waste to landfill

Forest Regeneration

We have planted over 60,000 native trees across our properties and buffer zones since 2019. Each guest stay contributes to the planting of 2 additional native trees.

60,000+Native trees planted

Community Employment

85% of our 320+ staff members are from villages within 20 km of each property. We pay above-market wages, provide housing assistance, and fund children's education.

Our Journey

The Aranyakaa Story

2018
The Discovery
Founders Rajan and Meera discover a 42-acre plot of degraded land in Ramanagara. The vision is born: restore the land, then invite the world to witness it.
2019
Club Aranyakaa Bangalore Opens
After 14 months of construction and ecological restoration, the flagship property opens with 6 cottages. Fully booked within the first month.
2020
Award & Recognition
Named Karnataka's Best Eco-Stay by Times of India Travel. Despite the pandemic, the property pivots to private family pods and records its best year for reviews.
2021
Expansion Begins
Club Aranyakaa Mandya opens on a 60-acre sugarcane farm. Simultaneously, construction begins on the Mysore heritage property.
2022
The Membership Launch
The Club Aranyakaa Membership program is launched. 200 founding members join within the first 60 days. RCI integration announced.
2023
Chennai & Goa
Two new properties open — our coastal Chennai retreat on ECR and the Goa property in North Goa. Total guest count crosses 5,000.
2024
South India's #1 Eco Resort
Awarded South India's Best Eco-Luxury Destination by Condé Nast Traveller India. 10,000+ guests. 60,000 trees planted across all properties.
2026
Coorg & Chikmagalur (Coming)
Two new highland properties in the coffee regions of Karnataka are in development. Pre-registration now open for founding members.
