Our Story

Built on a Dream, Grown with a Philosophy

From a single farmhouse in Ramanagara to India's most celebrated eco-luxury hospitality brand — the Aranyakaa story is one of purpose, passion, and an unwavering commitment to the land.

ARANYAKAA

Who We Are

Not Just a Resort.
A Movement.

Club Aranyakaa was born in 2019 from a simple belief: that luxury hospitality and environmental responsibility are not mutually exclusive. They can — and must — coexist.

What started as a single 5-acre eco-farm retreat in Ramanagara has grown into five distinct properties across Karnataka, Tamil Nadu, and Goa — but our core philosophy remains unchanged. Every structure we build, every meal we serve, and every experience we curate is guided by one question: "Are we leaving this land better than we found it?"

Today, we host over 10,000 guests annually, employ 250+ team members from local communities, and have planted over 50,000 trees across our properties. But numbers only tell part of the story.

Our Journey

The Aranyakaa Timeline

2019

The Beginning

Aranyakaa Farm Retreat opens its doors in Ramanagara with 5 acres, 6 rooms, and a big dream. Our founding philosophy: luxury and ecology as partners, never rivals.

2020

Growingroots

First IGBC certification received. Expanded to 20 acres, introduced organic farming, and launched our first farm-to-table restaurant. Named "Best Eco Retreat" by Lonely Planet.

2021

Beyond Karnataka

Second property opens in Mandya — our first farm-immersion retreat. Launched membership program with RCI integration, welcoming international guests.

2022

Heritage & Coast

Club Aranyakaa Mysore and Chennai properties launch. Crossed 5,000 guests milestone. Won South India's Leading Eco Resort at World Travel Awards.

2023

Expansion

Goa property joins the family. Planted our 50,000th tree. Launched Panchakarma detox programs and digital detox retreats. Featured in Condé Nast Traveller.

2024 – Present

The Movement Grows

Five properties, 250+ team members, 10,000+ annual guests. Launching our wellness academy and carbon-neutral roadmap to 2030.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

Environmental Stewardship

Every decision — from building materials to dining menus — is guided by our commitment to leave no trace. We are certified carbon-neutral by 2030.

🤝

Community First

80% of our team comes from local villages. We invest in local suppliers, support village schools, and share our land with community initiatives.

✨

Authentic Excellence

We never compromise on quality. From thread counts to culinary techniques — every detail is crafted with intention and care.

🧘

Holistic Well-being

True luxury is not just physical comfort — it's mental peace, spiritual connection, and emotional renewal. We design every experience for complete well-being.

🌍

Cultural Respect

We honor the local traditions, stories, and knowledge of every region we operate in. Our experiences are rooted in authentic cultural immersion.

🔬

Innovation

From solar technology to Ayurvedic formulations — we invest in research and innovation to push the boundaries of sustainable luxury hospitality.
