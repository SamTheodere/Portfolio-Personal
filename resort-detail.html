Our Flagship Property

A Living Sanctuary
in the Wilderness

Nestled across 42 lush acres in Ramanagara, Club Aranyakaa Bangalore is our flagship eco-luxury retreat. Surrounded by rocky hills, ancient forests, and a sprawling organic farm, this is where the demands of the city dissolve into the whispers of nature.

Every structure here has been built using locally sourced materials with minimal ecological impact. Solar energy powers the entire property, and our farm supplies over 80% of all ingredients used in our kitchen. This is luxury that gives back.

From our highest-rated Banyan Suite with its private plunge pool to the community bonfire clearing where strangers become friends — every corner of this resort has been designed with intention, beauty, and deep respect for nature.

Frequently Asked

Common Questions

Is Club Aranyakaa Bangalore suitable for families with young children?

+

Absolutely. We are one of Karnataka's most family-friendly eco-resorts. We have a dedicated kids zone, animal petting farm, shallow children's pool, nature workshops for kids aged 5–14, and a children's menu at all dining venues. Kids under 5 stay free.

What is included in the room rate?

+

All room rates include complimentary breakfast for the number of guests, access to all common amenities (pool, trails, farm, bonfire, yoga deck), Wi-Fi, and resort activities. Spa treatments, private dining, and specialty experiences are charged separately.

How far is the property from Bangalore city?

+

Club Aranyakaa Bangalore is located in Ramanagara, approximately 55 km from Bangalore city center. The drive takes about 60–75 minutes via NICE Road or Mysore Road. We offer complimentary airport and city transfers for guests — please book 48 hours in advance.

Can I host a wedding or private event at the property?

+

Yes. We have 8 unique event venues across the 42-acre property, including an outdoor amphitheatre, lakeside lawn, forest clearing, and decorated heritage hall. Our events team handles end-to-end planning. Capacity ranges from 20 to 300 guests depending on the venue.

Do you accommodate dietary requirements?

+

Fully. Our kitchen accommodates vegan, vegetarian, Jain, gluten-free, diabetic-friendly, and allergen-specific diets. Please inform us at booking so our chef team can ensure your meals are prepared with complete care and no cross-contamination.

Is Wi-Fi available throughout the property?

+

Yes, high-speed Wi-Fi is available in all rooms and common areas. However, we gently encourage guests — especially during our Digital Detox packages — to embrace "conscious disconnection" and experience the property without screens for designated hours each day.
