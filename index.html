01 — About

The Eye
Behind the
Lens

I'm a Senior Video Editor, Cinematographer, and Motion Graphics Lead based in Bengaluru. A rare arc from graphic design → motion graphics → full-scale production gives me 360° visual ownership that most editors can't offer.

My work has shaped the visual identity of India's most recognised developers — Birla Estates, Godrej Properties, Tata Housing, Embassy Group, Sobha, Purvankara, BNW Developments, Danube Properties, Binghatti, DAMAC, and Brigade Group. From aerial 8K shoots to Instagram-native creatives, I deliver broadcast-grade precision at every scale.

Not just an editor. A creative director with a camera. I don't cut footage — I build the visual language that makes brands impossible to ignore.
