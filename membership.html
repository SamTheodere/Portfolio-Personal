Questions

Membership FAQs

How long does it take to activate my membership?

+

Once your application is submitted, our team contacts you within 24 hours. Upon payment confirmation, your membership is activated immediately and your welcome kit is dispatched within 3–5 working days.

Can I upgrade my membership tier mid-year?

+

Yes, absolutely. You can upgrade at any time during your membership year. You'll only pay the prorated difference between your current tier and the new one. Downgrades take effect at the next renewal cycle.

Does membership cover stays for guests I bring with me?

+

Your membership discounts apply to your own booking. For the Gold plan (which covers couples), both you and your partner receive the benefits. Platinum covers a family of 4. Guests accompanying you beyond the tier coverage receive a 10% friend-of-member discount.

What happens if I already have an RCI membership?

+

Excellent — you'll love our RCI integration. Gold and Platinum members can link their RCI account to their Club Aranyakaa profile. This enables two-way exchange: redeem your RCI points for Aranyakaa stays, or bank your membership weeks into RCI points for use at 4,000+ resorts worldwide.

Is the membership refundable?

+

Within 7 days of purchase, you can request a full refund if you haven't yet used any membership benefits. After 7 days, the membership is non-refundable but fully transferable to another person upon request.

What are the member-only exclusive events?

+

Gold and Platinum members receive invitations to exclusive quarterly events — private farm dinners, seasonal harvest festivals, moonlight meditation gatherings, and our annual Aranyakaa Founders' Retreat. These are not available to public bookings at any price.
